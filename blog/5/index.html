Which one is correct, the map or the GPS trace

Discussion on methods for resolving discrepancies between GPS traces and map data in geospatial applications.

Authors: A Hendawi, SS Sabbineni, Jianwei Shen, Y Song, P Cao, Z Zhang, J Krumm, et al.

Published: 2019-11-05

Abstract

We investigate methods for determining the accuracy of GPS traces versus map data, providing algorithms and heuristics for resolving discrepancies in location-based applications.

Citation

Hendawi, A., Sabbineni, S.S., Shen, J., Song, Y., Cao, P., Zhang, Z., Krumm, J., et al. (2019). Which one is correct, the map or the GPS trace. Proceedings of the 27th ACM SIGSPATIAL International Conference on Advances in Geographic Information Systems.
